### What I do

I help small, local businesses turn their Google listing into customers. Most have great reviews and a phone number but no website — or a broken one. I build them a clean, quick-loading site that works on a phone, matches their brand, and plugs into their Google Business Profile so it actually shows up when people search.

Every build includes the things that quietly matter: local business schema so Google understands the site, click-to-call, a fast page that loads on a weak connection, and copy written for the customer, not for me.

### Featured project

**🍮 Yella Kunafa** — a kunafa & Middle Eastern dessert shop in northwest Edmonton
&nbsp;&nbsp;→ [**Live site**](https://bhargavthejs-sketch.github.io/Yella_Kunafe/) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Code](https://github.com/bhargavthejs-sketch/Yella_Kunafe)

A single-page site built around one goal: order &amp; pay for pickup while the kunafa is still hot. Real menu and prices, six verbatim Google reviews, a brand palette sampled from the shop's own sign, a catering lead form, and structured data (`Restaurant` + `Menu` schema) so Google can read the menu directly. No frameworks, no trackers — just fast HTML/CSS that loads in a car on LTE.

<sub>Built with: HTML5 · CSS (custom properties, responsive grid) · JSON-LD structured data · deployed on GitHub Pages</sub>
